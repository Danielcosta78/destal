Military-Grade Security

End-to-End Encryption

Destal uses WebRTC's built-in encryption combined with additional security layers to ensure your messages can only be read by the intended recipient.

Perfect Forward Secrecy

Each session generates unique encryption keys, preventing mass decryption even if a key is compromised.

No Metadata Collection

We don't store who you talk to or when, because we don't have servers that could store this information.
